About

The Committee is a 13-person mixed-gender a cappella group from the University of Waterloo. We started as a group of 9 friends coming up with choreography for ACE, another a cappella group on campus which all our members are current of past members of, as part of the "choreography committee," hence our name. We performed a song as a small group for the Winter 2014 End of Term Concert, and we have since grown to a diverse group of passionate singers and performers. We take pride on our focus on soul, R&B, and classic pop.
